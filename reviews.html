Common Criticisms

What reviewers flag

  • ▸
    Content volume

    The EA build covers roughly one-third of the planned game. Players who finish Chapter 1 hit a wall. More content arrives in Q3 2026.

  • ▸
    Mid-range performance

    Dense biomes, especially the Shallows kelp forests, cause frame drops on GPUs below the RTX 3080 tier. An optimization patch is targeted for June 2026.

  • ▸
    Base-building bugs

    Structural modules occasionally clip through geometry on steep slopes. A workaround is to build on flat substrate. A fix is in the next patch.

  • ▸
    Co-op desync

    Multiplayer sessions with high-latency connections (over 150ms) can produce creature position desync. The networking layer is listed as a known focus area.
