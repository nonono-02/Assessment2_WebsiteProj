The Story

You crash. You sink. You survive.

Your transport vessel, the Meridian, comes apart on re-entry over the ocean planet 4546C-II. You reach the surface in an emergency drop pod. The nearest landmass is 2,400 km away. Your distress beacon has a six-month battery. The ocean between you and rescue is alive.

Subnautica 2 builds on the loop of the original: scan, craft, dive deeper, survive. The new setting introduces a co-op cast — four survivors from the same wreck, each with a distinct backstory and starting inventory. You can find each other or go it alone.

The story unfolds through recovered ship logs, alien structures on the ocean floor, and a growing body of environmental evidence that the planet's ecosystem is not natural. Something seeded it. Something is still here.

Developer

Unknown Worlds Entertainment

Unknown Worlds is an independent studio based in San Francisco. They released the original Subnautica in 2018 and Below Zero in 2021. Subnautica 2 entered Early Access in May 2026 with a team of roughly 80 people.

The studio has published all its games through direct distribution on Steam and the Epic Games Store, keeping full ownership of the IP.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to play the original Subnautica first?

No. Subnautica 2 is set in a different ocean on a different planet. The story stands alone. Players who know the original will pick up some lore references, but nothing is required reading.

Is the Early Access build stable?

The current build is rated "Mostly Positive" on Steam. Major crashes are infrequent. Save corruption has been patched as of build 0.4.2. The developers post patch notes every two to three weeks.

Can I play solo?

Yes. The game is fully playable alone. Co-op is optional. Solo and multiplayer use the same world generation and content — nothing is locked behind co-op.

Will my Early Access save carry to full release?

Unknown Worlds has confirmed save compatibility for all builds from EA launch onward. Saves made before build 0.3.0 are not guaranteed to transfer.

Does the game have mod support?

Unofficial mods work through Nexus Mods now. Official mod tools are planned for the full release in 2027. The developer has said it will not break existing mod APIs between major updates.
